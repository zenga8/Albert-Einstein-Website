Theory of Relativity

Einstein's theory revolutionized the understanding of space, time, and gravity.

Special Relativity

Special relativity introduced the concept that time and space are relative and interwoven.

General Relativity

General relativity expanded on special relativity, describing gravity as the curvature of spacetime.

Impact and Applications

Relativity has profound implications in fields like astrophysics, GPS technology, and cosmology.
