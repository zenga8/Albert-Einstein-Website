Biography Details

Early Life

Albert Einstein was born in Ulm, Germany, in 1879.

Education and Early Career

Einstein studied physics at the Swiss Federal Polytechnic in Zurich.

Major Achievements

He published the theory of relativity and won the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921.

Personal Life

Einstein married Mileva Maric and later Elsa Löwenthal.

Later Years and Legacy

Einstein spent his later years in Princeton, New Jersey, and passed away in 1955.
